  MY OPEN SOURCE MANIFESTO
  Written on: 31 March 2026
========================================

I use Linux every single day without paying
for it. Someone built it, and then gave it away.
That's not normal -- that's a choice. A choice
that says knowledge should belong to everyone.

Freedom, to me, means Choice. And in software
that means being able to look inside the tools
I depend on -- not just use them blindly.

Linus Torvalds just wanted a free OS for his PC.
He shared it anyway. Now it runs the world.
If I ever build Coding tool, I'll share it too.
Because that's how I got everything I have.

-- Klemens Danny (24BCE10176), 31 March 2026
